Short Bio
  • I’m currently working towards Ph.D degree at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. My research interests mainly include cognitive science and machine learning.

  • It’s interesting to use novel machine learning algorithms for novel tasks such as mining/prediction/diagnosis in cognitive science or other fields. It’s more exciting to collaborate and discuss with researchers from different research fields. Hence, rather than limiting my interest to only one aspect, I am keeping my mind open to all possible interesting fundamental issues and applications relevant to cognitive science and machine learning.
