个人简介 Brief Introduction
  • 汤知日分别在2017和2019年于武汉大学获得学士和硕士学位，现在于香港城市大学计算机科学系攻读博士学位。近几年，在IEEE TCYB、TITS、TCDS和TED等知名期刊上发表多篇论文。曾获2018年 Intel InnovateFPGA全球创新大赛大中华区决赛银牌及一等奖。目前主要的研究兴趣包括认知科学、智慧医疗、生物医学信息学以及机器学习。
  • Zhi-Ri Tang received B.Sc and M.Eng degree from Wuhan University in 2017 and 2019, respectively. He is working towards Ph.D degree at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. In recent years, he has authored or co-authored many papers in refereed journals, including IEEE TCYB, TITS, TCDS, and TED. He has won silver medal and first prize in 2018 Intel InnovateFPGA Global Innovation Competition in Greater China Regional Final. His current research interests mainly include cognitive science, intelligent medicine, biomedical informatics, and machine learning.

研究兴趣 Research Interests
  • 方法层面：机器学习，数据挖掘，深度学习，图神经网络，以及面向缺少训练数据或标签的算法，包括半监督/无监督/弱监督/少样本学习等；
  • Methods: Machine learning, data mining, deep learning, graph neural network, and some algorithms aiming to solve the settings lack training data or labels, including semi-supervised/unsupervised/weakly supervised/few-shot learning;
  • 应用层面：认知科学，生物医学信息学，图像和信号处理，以及其他与智慧医疗相关的有趣应用。
  • Applications: Cognitive science, biomedical informatics, image and signal processing, and other interesting applications related to intelligent medicine.
