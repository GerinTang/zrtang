Brief Introduction
  • Zhi-Ri Tang received B.Sc and M.Eng degree from Wuhan University in 2017 and 2019, respectively. He is working towards Ph.D degree at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. His current research interests mainly include cognitive computing, intelligent healthcare, machine learning, biomedical informatics. In recent years, he has authored or co-authored many papers in refereed journals and conferences, including IEEE JBHI, TITS, TCDS, TED, etc. He also served as journal reviewers and conference PC, including IEEE TNNLS, TCYB, TII, etc. He has won silver medal and first prize in 2018 Intel InnovateFPGA Global Innovation Competition in Greater China Regional Final.
  • 汤知日分别在2017和2019年于武汉大学获得学士和硕士学位，现在于香港城市大学计算机科学系攻读博士学位。 主要的研究兴趣包括认知计算、智慧医疗、机器学习以及生物医学信息学。 近年来，在IEEE JBHI、TITS、TCDS和TED等权威SCI期刊/国际会议上发表多篇论文，同时也是IEEE TNNLS、TCYB、TII等多个期刊审稿人/会议程序委员会成员。 曾获2018年Intel InnovateFPGA全球创新大赛大中华区决赛银牌及一等奖。

Research Interests
  • Methods: Machine learning, data mining, deep learning, graph neural network, and some algorithms aiming to solve the settings lack training data or labels, including semi-supervised/unsupervised/weakly supervised/few-shot learning;
  • Applications: Cognitive science, biomedical informatics, image and signal processing, and other interesting applications related to intelligent healthcare.
