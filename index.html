Brief Introduction
  • Zhi-Ri Tang received B.Sc and M.Eng degree from Wuhan University in 2017 and 2019, respectively. He is working towards Ph.D degree at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. In recent years, he has authored or co-authored many papers in refereed journals, including IEEE TCYB, TCDS, TED, etc. He has won silver medal and first prize in 2018 Intel InnovateFPGA Global Innovation Competition in Greater China Regional Final. His current research interests mainly include cognitive computing, brain-computer interface, machine learning, intelligent healthcare, biomedical informatics.

Research Interests
  • Methods: Machine learning, data mining, deep learning, graph neural network, and some algorithms aiming to solve the settings lack training data or labels, including semi-supervised/unsupervised/weakly supervised/few-shot learning;
  • Applications: Cognitive science, brain-computer interface, biomedical informatics, image and signal processing, and other interesting applications related to intelligent healthcare.
