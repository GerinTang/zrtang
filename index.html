个人简介 Brief Introduction
  • 汤知日分别在2017和2019年于武汉大学获得学士和硕士学位，现在于香港城市大学计算机科学系攻读博士学位。 主要的研究兴趣包括认知工效学、生物医学信息学以及机器学习。 近几年在知名期刊上发表多篇文章，包括IEEE TCYB，TITS，TCDS，以及TED。 曾获2018年 Intel InnovateFPGA全球创新大赛大中华区决赛一等奖及银牌。
  • Zhi-Ri Tang received B.Sc and M.Eng degree from Wuhan University in 2017 and 2019, respectively. He is working towards Ph.D degree at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. His research interests mainly include cognitive ergonomics, biomedical informatics, and machine learning. In recent years, he has authored or co-authored many papers in refereed journals, including IEEE TCYB, TITS, TCDS, and TED. He has won first prize and silver medal in 2018 Intel InnovateFPGA Global Innovation Competition in Greater China Regional Final.
